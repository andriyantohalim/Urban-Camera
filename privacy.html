Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

Our app collects the following data when you use the camera functionalities:

  • Location Data: If you enable the 'Save Location' option, the app will store the geographic location where each photo is taken.
  • Usage Data: The app may collect data on how you interact with various features, such as the shutter count, to improve the user experience.
  • Photo Metadata: Metadata associated with the photos, such as the lens used (0.5x, 1.0x, 2.0x), and whether the front camera was mirrored, may be saved.

2. Data Usage

The data we collect is used solely for the purpose of enhancing your experience with our camera app:

  • Location Data: Used to tag photos with the location information, allowing you to see where your photos were taken.
  • Usage Data: Helps us understand which features are most popular and how to improve the app in future updates.
  • Photo Metadata: Allows us to provide you with accurate details about your photos, including lens and camera settings.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except under the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may disclose your data to authorities.
  • Service Providers: We may share anonymized data with service providers to help improve our app's performance.

4. Data Security

We take your data security seriously. All data collected is stored securely using industry-standard encryption protocols:

  • Location Data: Stored securely on your device and not shared with third parties.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized and encrypted before being stored or shared.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide you with the services and features offered by our app:

  • Location Data: Retained as part of your photos' metadata until you choose to delete the photo.
  • Usage Data: Retained in an anonymized form for statistical analysis.

6. User Control and Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Preserve Settings: You can choose to preserve or reset your app settings at any time.
  • Delete Data: You can delete individual photos, including their location data and metadata, directly from the app.
  • Data Access: You can request access to view or delete your data by contacting us through the app's support page.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you by updating the policy on this page and revising the 'Last Updated' date at the top.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the support page in the app or via email at support@urbancam.com.
